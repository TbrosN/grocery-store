🚫 AVOID LIKE THE PLAGUE (Absolute Dealbreakers)
If a product hits any of these thresholds or contains any of these ingredients, it is highly processed or inflammatory. Put it back.

The "Red Flag" Nutrition Facts:

Added Sugars > 0g (Zero tolerance for added sweeteners)

Trans Fat > 0g (Zero tolerance for artificial fats)

Carb-to-Fiber Ratio > 10:1 (Highly refined grains that will spike blood sugar)

Sodium > 20% DV (> 470mg) (Massive red flag for ultra-processed food)

Saturated Fat > 20% DV (> 4g) (In packaged foods, this usually indicates heavy processing)

The "Red Flag" Ingredients:

Industrial Oils: Partially hydrogenated oils, corn, soybean, cottonseed oil

Sweeteners: High-fructose corn syrup, aspartame, sucralose

Toxic Preservatives: Sodium nitrate/nitrite, BHA, BHT

Artificial Dyes: Red 40, Yellow 5, Yellow 6

Gut-Disrupting Gums: Carrageenan, xanthan, guar, cellulose, locust bean, gellan, gum arabic

⚠️ SOMETIMES FOR SOME PEOPLE (Eat in Moderation)
These are for foods that don't have the toxic ingredients above, but still require portion control due to glycemic impact, cardiovascular strain, or mild processing (e.g., natural red meat, white rice, whole grain pasta).

The "Moderation" Nutrition Facts:

Carb-to-Fiber Ratio between 5:1 and 10:1 (Moderate glycemic impact; ideal is under 5:1)

Sodium between 10% and 20% DV (230mg - 470mg)

Saturated Fat between 10% and 20% DV (2g - 4g) (Typical of a reasonable red meat serving)

Cholesterol > 20% DV (> 60mg) (Usually found in organ meats/eggs; natural but should be tracked)
